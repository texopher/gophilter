You can create, view, and edit Google documents, as well as Microsoft Word®
files, with the Google Docs app for Android.

===== Step 1: Download the Google Docs app =====

===== Step 2: Get started =====

On your Android phone or tablet, open a document in the Google Docs app . Tap
Edit . To select a word, double-tap it. Move the blue markers to select more
text. Start editing. To undo or redo an action, click Undo or Redo .

===== Work with different file formats =====

You can upload and download files with the Google Docs app for Android.

===== Step 3: Share & work with others =====

You can share files and folders with people and choose whether they can view,
edit, or comment on them.
